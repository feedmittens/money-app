Tally

A self-hosted personal finance tracker. Your data stays in your PostgreSQL database, on your hardware, under your control.

View on GitHub Read the Manual
🏦

Account Register

Checking, savings, credit, and investment accounts with running balances.

📋

Bills & Income

Recurring entries with flexible frequencies — monthly, weekly, custom days.

📊

Budget & Reports

Monthly budgets by category, spending reports, and tax-export support.

🔮

Balance Forecast

Project your balance up to 36 months forward based on bills and income.

📥

Import

Drag-and-drop QIF, OFX/QFX, and CSV import with preview before commit.

🔒

Multi-user Auth

Session auth with bcrypt, TOTP 2FA, Google OAuth, and admin approval.
